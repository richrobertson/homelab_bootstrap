# Code of Conduct

This repository is maintained as a public collaboration space. Participation requires professional, respectful behavior.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating
- Keep feedback specific to code, documentation, or process
- Share enough context for others to reproduce or understand a problem
- Respect maintainer time and repository scope

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or demeaning language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Spamming, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Pressuring maintainers for support outside the documented channels

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately using the contact details published on the repository profile. Do not use public issues for conduct reports.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not meet this standard.
